History Of Character

Super Mario is one of the most iconic platformer in gaming history

mario is created by Japanese game designer Shigreu Miyamoto

the first time that mario shown in the video games was in Donkey Knog franchaise and the mascot of the Japanese video game company Nintendo. Mario is an Italian plumber who lives in the Mushroom Kingdom with his younger twin brother, Luigi. Their adventures generally involve rescuing Princess Peach from the villain Bowser while using power-ups that give them different abilities. Mario's distinctive characteristics include his large nose and mustache, overalls, red cap, and high-pitched, exaggerated Italian accent.
